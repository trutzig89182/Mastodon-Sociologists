Sociologists on Mastodon

Follow all your favourite sociologists

You want to get in touch with sociologists from around the world on Mastodon? This page lists accounts from sociologists and sociological institutions and offers an easy method to follow multiple accounts at once. Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find an short instruction, on how to do this.

Accounts by handle, name, and profile link (most recent first)

Full names are only included if they are shown in the profile or on explicit demand. You want to be on the list, too? Please use this form (preferred), send me an email david.adler@uni-oldenburg.de or contact me under @perspektivbrocken@social.tchncs.de.

You are interested in sociological content on mastodon, but you don’t want to be added to the list? Then you could consider follwing the group @sociology@a.gup.pe.
