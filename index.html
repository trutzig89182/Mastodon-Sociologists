Sociologists on Mastodon

Follow all your favourite sociologists

You want to get in touch with sociologists from around the world on Mastodon? This page lists accounts from sociologists and sociological institutions and offers an easy method to follow multiple accounts at once. Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find a short instruction, on how to do this.

Further information on how to be added or removed can be found at the bottom of this page.

Clicking on a keyword or language allows you to filter the list; clicking the activated keyword/language again will undo the filter.

Accounts by handle, name, keywords, and main language(s) (most recent first)

Full names are only included if they are shown in the profile or on explicit demand. You want to be on the list, too? Please use this form (preferred), send me an email david.adler@uni-oldenburg.de or contact me under @perspektivbrocken@social.tchncs.de.

You are interested in sociological content on mastodon, but you don’t want to be added to the list? Then you could consider following the group @sociology@a.gup.pe.

If you want to be removed from the list, you can contact me in any way.
